What happens next
1
Download Smith Cut Get the installer from the download page. The .dmg installs like any Mac app.
2
Run setup.command Double-click the setup script. It installs Python 3.11, FFmpeg, and the Premiere Link extension automatically — takes about 3 minutes.
3
Activate your license Open Smith Cut, go to Settings, paste your SMITH-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX key. You're active immediately.
4
Open Premiere → Window → Extensions → Pymiere Link This one manual step connects Smith Cut to Premiere. Do it once per session.
