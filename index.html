The pipeline

FOUR STEPS.
ONE CLICK.

01
Beat Detection
Librosa analyzes your music and finds every beat, onset, and rhythmic hit. 103 BPM? 168 cut points mapped to the millisecond.
02
Clip Metadata
FFprobe reads every clip — duration, frame rate, codec, creation date. Sort by name, date, size, or let the AI decide.
03
Beat Matching
Each clip is assigned to a beat slot and trimmed to exactly that duration. Loop, shuffle, vary — full control over pacing.
04
Premiere Import
Every clip placed on the Premiere timeline at its exact beat position via pymiere. Music track placed. Project saved.
