The pipeline

FOUR STEPS.
ONE CLICK.

01
Beat Detection
Librosa analyzes your music locally — no upload needed. Finds every beat, onset, and rhythmic hit. Energy levels mapped per beat for smart placement.
02
Clip Metadata
FFprobe reads every clip on your local drive — duration, frame rate, codec, creation date. Your footage path never leaves your machine.
03
Beat Matching
Each clip is assigned to a beat slot and trimmed to exactly that duration. Loop, shuffle, vary — full control over pacing.
04
Premiere Import
Every clip placed on the Premiere timeline at its exact beat position. Music track placed. Project saved. Total time: under 60 seconds.
